# Seed & Serve — Editor Guide (Non-Technical)

## Getting Started
1. Go to `/auth/signin` and log in with your Editor credentials.
2. You'll land on the **Admin Dashboard**.

## Creating a Campaign
1. Click **"New Campaign"** in the top right.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Clear, compelling name
   - **Slug**: Auto-generated URL (e.g., `feed-children-2026`)
   - **Summary**: 1–2 sentence teaser (max 500 chars)
   - **Goal Amount**: Target fundraising amount in INR
   - **Tags**: Comma-separated (e.g., `food, children, education`)
   - **Featured Image**: Upload or paste URL
   - **Content**: Rich text editor — add paragraphs, bullets, etc.
3. Set **Status** to `DRAFT` while editing.
4. Click **Publish** (set Status → `PUBLISHED`) to go live.

## Viewing Donations
1. Click **Donations** in the sidebar.
2. See donor name, email, amount, campaign, and date.
3. Click **Export CSV** to download all donor data.

## Publishing an Impact Story
1. Go to **Impact Stories** (if visible in sidebar).
2. Click **New Story**, fill title, content, and images.
3. Set **Published At** date and save.

## Tips
- **Preview** your campaign before publishing with the Preview button.
- Images should be at least 1200×630px for best display.
- Contact `admin@seedandserve.org` for account issues.
